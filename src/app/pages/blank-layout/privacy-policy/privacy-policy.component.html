Privacy Policy for EHA Guide Mobile App

Effective Date: December 04, 2024

Introduction:

This Privacy Policy describes how our team uses and protects your personal information when you use our mobile application, EHA Guide. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring that your personal information is handled responsibly and in accordance with applicable data protection laws.

Information We Collect:

  • Full name: We collect your full name for identification and personalization purposes.
  • Email address: Your email address is used for account creation, password recovery, and to send you important information about the app and updates.
  • Password: We store your password securely to protect your account from unauthorized access.
  • Phone number: Your phone number is required to contact you whenever needed.
  • Job title: We use your job title to tailor content and recommendations within the app.
  • Workplace: We use your workplace to tailor content and recommendations as well.

How We Use Your Information:

  • To provide and operate EHA: We use your information to create and manage your account, personalize your experience, and provide you with access to the app's features and functionality.
  • To send important information: We may use your email address to send you important information about the app, such as updates, security alerts, and support messages.
  • To improve the app: We may use your information to analyze usage trends, understand user preferences, and improve the overall functionality and content of the app.
  • To personalize your experience: We may use your job title and workplace to tailor content and recommendations within the app to make it more relevant to your needs.

Sharing Your Information:

We will not share your personal information with any third-party companies or individuals without your consent. However, there may be circumstances where we are required to disclose your information, such as:

  • To comply with the law: We may disclose your information if required by law or in response to a valid legal request.
  • To protect our rights: We may disclose your information if necessary to protect our rights or the rights of others, such as in the case of fraud or abuse.

Data Security:

We take reasonable measures to ensure the security of your personal information. We use industry-standard encryption technologies to protect your data from unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction. However, no security measure is perfect, and we cannot guarantee the absolute security of your information.

Your Choices:

  • Access your information: You can access and update your personal information at any time through your account settings in the app.
  • Delete your information: You can request to delete your account and all of your personal information at any time. To do so, please contact us at bonder@digitalbondmena.com.
  • Delete your account: You can permanently delete or deactivate your account at any time from your profile settings.

Changes to this Policy:

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the updated Policy on our website or within the app. We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy regularly for any changes.

Contact Us:

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at bonder@digitalbondmena.com.
